About Us

Safety Before Firearms is committed to protecting the rights of law-abiding citizens to keep and bear arms. We believe that with rights come responsibilities, and we advocate for sensible safety measures and control policies that respect individual freedoms.

It's crucial to recognize and consider diverse viewpoints when discussing gun control. People may have different experiences, beliefs, and concerns regarding firearms, and engaging in constructive dialogue allows us to find common ground and develop effective policies that balance individual rights with public safety.
